About Us

HISTORY

Manbase is an all-in-one HRIS software solution designed to simplify and streamline HR processes for businesses of all sizes. Our journey began in 2010 with the goal of providing an affordable and easy-to-use HRIS solution for small and medium-sized businesses. Over the years, we have expanded our reach and now serve companies across industries and sizes, always maintaining our commitment to exceptional customer service and support.

Our team of dedicated software developers, HR experts, and customer service representatives work collaboratively to continuously improve and innovate our HRIS software. We prioritize ease of use, security, and compliance with labor laws and regulations to ensure our customers have a reliable and efficient HR system.

FAQs

1What is MANBASE Solutions?

MANBASE is a Human Resource Information System (HRIS), which is software that automates and manages HR processes such as payroll, benefits administration, performance management, and employee data management. The benefits of an HRIS for a company include improved efficiency, reduced errors, and better compliance with regulations.

2What features does your software offer?

MANBASE software offers features such as payroll processing, benefits administration, time and attendance tracking, performance management, employee self-service, and reporting/analytics.

3What security measures do you have in place to protect my data?

We have multiple layers of security measures in place, including encryption, firewalls, access controls, and regular backups. We also comply with industry-standard security certifications.

4What kind of support and training do you offer for your MANPOWER software?

We offer a range of support and training options, including online resources, user guides, webinars, and phone/email support. We also provide on-site training and implementation support as needed.

5How customizable is your HRIS software to fit our specific needs?

Our MANBASE software is highly customizable and can be tailored to meet your company's specific needs and workflows.
